TasteHealthy-API

Jan - May 2017, Individual work

TasteHealthy is a mobile app for easy logging and recommendation of tasty and healthy food. During my exchange in NUS, I contributed to this project, gained practical experience in using food recognition and recommendation algorithms, developed a RESTful API by Flask to evaluate users' health conditions, calculate target nutrient and retrieve food data, and implemented an advanced function of nutrition value interval retrieve based on Nutritionix and HPBFOCOS. link

AI Report

Nov 2016 - Mar 2017, Individual work

AI Report Edition is a project led by Dr. Hongxin Zhang, State Key Lab of CAD & CG, ZJU, aimed at developing AI algorithms to generate journal reports on different areas semi-automatically for governmental decisions. I collected data by Scrapy from influential media, and extracted topics by word frequency counting and natural language processing using LDA and word2vec algorithms. With the collected data, I made interactive heatmaps based on China and world maps to indicate the popularity of a certain topic in a region, along with hottest key words.
