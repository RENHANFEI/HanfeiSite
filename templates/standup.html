StandUp

A Desktop application to stop office workers from sedentary lifestyle.

Summary


To alleviate current-, and prevent future complications that result from improper posture at the office due to sedentary lifestyle, we designed StandUp, a desktop app providing a new work ethic that allows for small exercises and/or breaks during work time.

Background


It is a project done in a workshop held by International Design Institute of Zhejiang University, De Technische Universiteit Eindhoven and Philips, focused on wicked problems. My group was assigned to focus on sub-healthy problems of office workers.

Process

At the first stage, we did some online search, to find that limited physical exercise and lack of sound sleep are two most serious problems for office workers. To consolidate the statement, we collected data from WeRun, MiBand, a self-made Arduino mat and questionnaires.

Questionnarie

Workload
Sleep
Exercise

Many office workers suffer from heavy workload since they work more than 7.5 hours when 6 is usually recommended. Meanwhile, they do not have sufficient sleep nor proper physical exercise.

Data from WeRun, MiBand, Arduino mat

  • According to WeRun, office workers usually have much fewer steps than young students.
  • In term of MiBand, most of the subject's sleep is somnolence, which infers undesirable sleeping state.
  • In light of data from Arduino mat, our subject sits all the day with rare physical exercise.

It was demonstrated that office workers tend to have unhealthy long working and sitting hours and insufficient exercise so that we decided to concentrate on sedentary lifestyle problems.In the following interviews, we focused on interviewing office workers. By doing further interviews on them, we had much more clear user profiles.

User Profiles

My boss shows no sympathy of our working disease.

My colleagues suffer from shoulder periarthritis, rhinitis caused by inactivity.

I concentrate on my work and forget to leave my seat except going to the bathroom.

We asked for professionals' advice, who told us low-effort physical exercise have great positive impact on health. It inspired us that we could encourage low-effort exercise to improve office workers' health condition.


To visualize our products, we investigated the market to find some competitors including massage products, cushions, and apps, and made a report from market perspective.

Market Research

  • There is an increasing popularity of sports products around the world like smart bands and sports apps.
  • People are paying more attention to health problems and are willing to pay for their health care.
  • Sedentary problem has not become a noticing problem.
  • Facing sedentary problem, people are just concerned about the bone problems which makes most consumers buy the products for the elderly.

After the analysis, we got our journey map meanwhile decided features of our product. The basic idea is to remind the long-sitting office workers to do small exercise at set intervals. To achieve our target, we have to convince the employers, to stimulate the employees, and to let the employees encourage each other. We did brainstorming to include reminder with sensor, QR code, company culture, bonus, etc as our product's possible features.

Journey Map

Design


Finally, we decided to design a desktop app that reminds the office workers to stand up and walk around at certain intervals. A small collection game is also added, in which the employee can scan to get a piece of puzzle in staff room at break time, exchange with coworkers and complete a puzzle to gain some points.

We set two revenues for economics.

Along with one teammate, I made prototype for basic interfaces, and shot a sample video.
