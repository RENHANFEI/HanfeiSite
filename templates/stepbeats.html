StepBeats

An artificial intelligence platform for creating music through exercise.

Summary


StepBeats is a mobile application aimed to help users produce music from running. We combined the ideas of healthcare applications, UGC, Computer generated X (X = picture, video, music, etc.) together to achieved StepBeats and relevant products.

Award: Gold Prize, "Internet Plus" National College Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Competition.

Background


There are three eye-catching trends in different fields leading nowadays fashion.

  • Health has become a deep concern for everybody, when running, along with running Apps, gets prevalent and turns into a new fashion.
  • UGC mode has gained remarkable achievement.
  • Computer generated X (X = picture, video, music, etc.) develops well.

We came up with the idea of StepBeats, which combined health-care, UGC and computer generated music together for brand new interesting experience.
